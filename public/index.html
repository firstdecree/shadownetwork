What and Why

Understanding the core mission of ShadowNetwork.

What is ShadowNetwork?

ShadowNetwork is a privacy-focused, zero-knowledge platform designed to securely and effortlessly store your contacts. With ShadoWnetwork, you can store and connect your contacts with ease.

Why use it?

ShadowNetwork is open-source and privacy-focused, designed for ease of use. You only need a username and password to get started, making it simple to access your account without the risk of forgetting complex credentials.

Why ShadowNetwork?

Ultimate Security

We follow a zero-knowledge, privacy-focused policy, ensuring that only you can access your data. We use advanced encryption to guarantee its security.

Open-Source

Our entire platform is open-source. Anyone can verify our encryption methods, review the code for vulnerabilities, and contribute to our platform's transparency and security.

Indefinite Retention

Never lose a contact again. Our backup system and resilient data storage ensure your network remains secure and accessible for a lifetime. However, we cannot guarantee 100% protection as human error is always a possibility.
